# News Compliance & Aggregation Strategy: Arabia Khaleej

## 📋 Objective
To provide real-time regional updates while strictly adhering to the **Cybercrime Prevention Law (No. 14 of 2014)** and local media regulations in Qatar and the GCC.

## 🛡️ Legal Safeguards
To minimize risk as a hobbyist platform, the following measures are implemented:

1. **Official-Only Sourcing**:
   - We aggregate exclusively from official state news agencies (QNA, WAM, SPA, BNA, ONA).
   - We do not use third-party "independent" news sites or blogs which may carry unverified information.

2. **Verbatim Content**:
   - Content is displayed as-is from the RSS feed.
   - **Strictly No Original Reporting**: We do not write our own news or interpret events.
   - **No Editorial Commentary**: We do not provide opinions on news items.

3. **Direct Redirection**:
   - Every headline is a direct link to the official agency's portal. The platform acts as a "terminal" (utility), not a "media outlet" (publisher).

4. **Transient Data**:
   - No permanent database storage of news content. Data is fetched in real-time or from a short-term cache.

## 🌏 Community & Expat Feeds
To serve the diverse demographic of the GCC, the platform includes official national feeds from major expat home countries:
- **India**: ANI (National) & Regional Hindi feeds.
- **Pakistan**: APP (Official) & BBC Urdu.
- **Bangladesh**: BSS (Official) & Bengali feeds.
- **Philippines**: PNA (Official) & Filipino bulletins.

These are categorized as "Community Updates" to distinguish them from local GCC state bulletins.

## ⚖️ Disclaimer
Arabia Khaleej is an independent digital reference. It is not affiliated with any government body. All news content is provided for informational purposes only. Information should be verified with the respective official news agencies.
